About Us

About The Seven Seeds Uganda

The Seven Seeds is a youth organization located in Lira Uganda. Its name came from the seven inital people that started. These are young people ensuring Building Dreams and Restoration of Hope in youths and adults around their region through practical steps of sensitization, creating awareness on different matters and in helping young people unlock the potentials of Greatness in them.

We Recognize the needs of the youths and adults with their untapped potentials, aiming to address their spiritual, mental, academic and financial needs and the needs of their community while enabling them to overcome challenges faced in addressing such needs.

Why Choose Us?

Through the help of well wishers and volunteer, we are able to exercise and accomplish our different task God has put before us. you too can be part of the Story God is writing in the hearts of His people.

History about The Seven Seeds

The Seven Seeds is an organization which started in 2019 headed by Otim Pius Jerry. The union of The Seven Seeds (3 girls 4 boys) is believed to be so extra ordinary because of how God brought them together. It was truly God’s calling because the members came from different backgrounds not even friends before and turned out into brothers and sisters with the same vision.

The Union of the Seven people later turned out to be more than just getting together but the union to make a difference in their socieites. The Seeds have one common belief that God choose the seven to start but the number of the seeds growing are unlimited compared to the seven pioneers
