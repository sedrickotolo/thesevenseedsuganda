About Us

The Seven Seeds is a youth led organization located in Lira City Uganda. Its name came from the seven inital people who were inspired to create impact in the lives of youths but has birthed to hundreds of seeds.

These are young people ensuring Building Dreams and Restoration of Hope in youths and adults around their region through practical steps of sensitization, creating awareness on different matters and in helping young people unlock the potentials of Greatness in them to become the people God wants them to be.

We Recognize the needs of the youths with their untapped potentials, aiming to address their spiritual, mental, academic and financial needs and the needs of their community while enabling them to overcome challenges faced in addressing such needs.

We are moving closely to our communities in various ways so that create long lasting impacts through weekly radiop programs, music, church outreaches, community visits, writing inspirational and christian books and devotionals and holding business sessions with the youths.
