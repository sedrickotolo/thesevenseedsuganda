About Champion of Change 2021

About The Conference

The Seven Seeds 2nd Annual Champion of Change Conference under the Theme Creativity, Innovation and Entrepreneurship for a better tomorrow. The conference is slated to take place on the 27th November, 2021 at Grand Pacific Hotel Lira from 10am to 4pm. The Champion of Change Conference is an Annual conference organized by The Seven Seeds Uganda

Today’s youth are tomorrow’s drivers of entrepreneurial activity in their communities, regions and countries. An increasing number of governments worldwide recognize the importance of investing in an entrepreneurship culture among young people, a crucial foundation for any sustainable and dynamic private sector.

Register to Attend
Why The Conference

How can education systems, innovative ideas and the private sector most effectively promote entrepreneurial attitudes and talent in youth? In order to address this question, The Champion of Change Conference is envisioned to develop and nurture youths to be creative, innovative and have good entrepreneurial skills for a better living tomorrow with a targeted number of 150 youths in attendance under creativity, Innovation and Entrepreneurship.

The Conference’s diverse and dynamic group of speakers, panelists, youths, education officials and practitioners provided an in-depth insight and refreshing wealth of perspectives, which will lead to new approaches in fostering entrepreneurial youth within the Lango Region.

Developing and nurturing youths to be creative, innovative and have good entrepreneurial skills for a better living tomorrow.

Expected addressable discussions.

  • How can entrepreneurial activities contribute to a country’s economic transformation?
  • How can the private sector contribute to improving the quality of entrepreneurship education and training?
  • How can Youth grow in Creativity, be innovative and become key players in the development of the region and their communities.

Expected Outputs.

  • Developing training, coaching and mentoring for youth entrepreneurs
  • Building Entrepreneurial among youths networks in supporting entrepreneurship as sources of motivation, ideas, information, advice, business partners, employees, and customers.
  • The key ingredients of training, coaching and mentoring for youth entrepreneurs and building successful youth entrepreneurship programmes.
