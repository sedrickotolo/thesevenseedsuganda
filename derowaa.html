DEROWAA

Derowaa

Traditional Fashion Event

This is a programme designed by The seven seeds Uganda to help bridge the gap between the church and the youth through organizing different campaigns that offer the young people an opportunity to draw closer to the church and attain positive transformation. It was also instigated to help revive the love for the African Culture and Tradition as its one of the ways through which we can build a strong brotherhood. Today, majority of the young people have been misled into destructive acts like drug abuse and sexual immorality among others. Because of this, a good number of them have ended up either pregnant, infected with STD’s, dropped out of school or even homeless and on the streets which really aren’t safe.

As the saying goes, ‘if the mountain can`t come to you, go to the mountain’ we therefore organize events like Music Dance and Drama, Fashion shows etc to enable as attract these vulnerable youths.We move out to the slums, markets, dance halls, discos, schools, churches and the general community with our main goal being winning more souls to Christ. By this we get to meet them, discuss alot about how best we can be of help so as to make them the better people the nation needs.
